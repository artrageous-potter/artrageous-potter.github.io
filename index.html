About

Artrageous Potter is a contemporary paint-your-own pottery studio located in Atlanta, GA. We provide a creative fun space for anyone to create your own work of art. Walk-ins are welcome!


No studio fees!

All prices include paint, glazing, and firing. You only pay for the piece that you pick, simple!

Pottery Painting Guide

Choose from over 500 unpainted ceramic pieces

Select your colors and paint however you like

FAQ

Do we need to make an appointment?

We welcome walk-ins, larger groups may require notice.

When will I get my piece back?

Turn around is guaranteed about 7 days, we can let you know if sooner.

How long are painted pieces kept for?

Our policy is 120 days and customers are called with reminders.

How old do you have to be to paint?

All ages are welcomed.

Is my piece food safe and microwavable?

Pieces are food safe, but not recommended for microwave or dishwasher.

Do you do shipping?

We offer shipping, the expected shipping takes approximately 7-14 days.

Do you offer discounts?

We have loyalty rewards and weekday specials. For every $2.00 spent you'll receive a star, and every 100 stars receive $20 off the next purchase.

Do you do birthday parties?

We offer birthday parties, corporate team-building events, mommy and me classes, girl scout events, school field trips, and kids summer camp.
